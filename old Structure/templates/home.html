Welcome to Naari

Your go-to website for all things women essentials.

Naari is an empowering platform dedicated to women's safety, health, and well-being. Seamlessly combining innovation and accessibility, Naari offers features like self-defense techniques, menstrual health insights, and a gynecologist finder for personalized care. Navigate through tools to know your legal rights, find nearby NGOs, file complaints effortlessly, or book a safe cab with ease. With interactive elements like a memorial wall to honor changemakers, Naari not only provides essential resources but also fosters a sense of community and empowerment. Explore, learn, and act—all in one intuitive space designed to make a difference.

Who are we?

Welcome to Naari, a project born from passion, purpose, and the unwavering belief in creating a safer world for women. We are a team of four young women pursuing our Master’s in Informatics, united by a shared dream: to use technology as a tool for empowerment and change.

Through Naari, we aim to foster a supportive community where women can share their experiences, access crucial resources, and find their voices. From connecting users with vital helplines to offering tools for personal safety and well-being, our platform is built with the real needs of women in mind.

Together, we hope to inspire change, spark conversations, and build a world where every woman can walk with confidence and dignity. Join us in this journey to make safety and empowerment a reality for all.
